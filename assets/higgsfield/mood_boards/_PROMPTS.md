## Werkstatt-modern (Direction 1)

### 01_werkstatt_modern_a_shk_workshop.png
> Documentary-style photograph of a German SHK (plumbing/heating) craftsman's workshop. Medium-wide shot of a wall organized with tools — wrenches, pipe cutters, copper fittings hanging on perforated steel boards. Diffuse industrial overhead lighting, slight haze, depth of field with the foreground tools sharp. Aesthetic reference: Festool / Miele Professional brand photography. Industrial blue accents on tool handles, anthracite-painted walls, slightly worn but well-maintained. No people in frame. Reportage feel — like a Stern or Geo magazine spread about German trades. Authentic, lived-in, not staged. 35mm focal length, natural color grading, no overprocessing.

### 02_werkstatt_modern_b_hand_drill.png
> Documentary close-up photograph: a craftsman's hand reaching for a Festool-style cordless drill on a workbench. Industrial blue rubber grip on the tool, copper pipes faintly visible in the soft-focus background. Shallow depth of field, hand sharp, background bokeh. Warm fluorescent shop lighting, slight grain. Aesthetic: Miele Professional brand photography meets Stern magazine reportage. Anthracite gray workbench surface, brass fittings, no logos visible. Authentic German craftsman workshop, slightly worn, never staged.

### 03_werkstatt_modern_c_workbench_detail.png
> Detail shot, documentary style: a wooden workbench corner with a metal vise clamp, an old Moleskine-style notebook with handwritten German notes (Auftragsnummer, Kunde, Zeit), a pencil, and a few copper pipe fittings scattered. Soft north-facing window light from camera-left, slight haze. Earthy industrial palette — muted gray, oxidized copper, warm wood, anthracite metal. Reportage feel like a Geo magazine spread about traditional German trades meeting modern operations. Authentic, slightly chaotic but organized, lived-in. Wide-angle 35mm.

## Berlin-Direct (Direction 3)

### 07_berlin_direct_a_altbau_team.png
> Documentary editorial photograph: a Berlin Altbau apartment building exterior, classic late-19th-century facade with stucco details, ornate balconies, slightly weathered. Two cleaning service workers in dark uniforms with subtle company branding (no readable logo) walking away from a parked white work-van toward the entrance. Late afternoon Berlin light, slight golden cast on the facade. BVG-style direct, unposed, journalistic feel. Wide angle 28mm, full scene. Aesthetic: Stern magazine reportage about Berlin trades. High contrast, real city, real light.

### 08_berlin_direct_b_sbahn_platform.png

Aesthetic: BVG-poster style direct documentary, Stern magazine sensibility. Subtle high contrast, real Berlin urban scene, kantig. 50mm. No logos, no text on clothing.
